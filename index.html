Smart home

Smart home that feels invisible, not overwhelming

Smart home should make life smoother, not more complicated. Handy Duo integrates devices into your existing home in a way that feels natural, stable, and unobtrusive.

From doorbells and locks to lighting and sensors, the goal is simple: technology that quietly supports how you already live. No cluttered wiring, no chaotic apps, no half‑finished installs.

  • Video doorbells and cameras
  • Smart locks and access
  • Smart lighting and dimmers
  • Wi‑Fi and mesh optimization
  • Device replacement and cleanup
  • Cable management and mounting

If you’re not sure what you need, describe how you want the home to feel and function—solutions can be tailored around that, not just the hardware.

Smart home work is approached like any other finish detail—clean, intentional, and integrated into the space.
